# Sophia - Biography

## Executive Summary

Sophia is the flagship AI Employee of Workforce AI and the primary representative of the platform.

She specializes in enterprise sales conversations, customer engagement, insurance product recommendations, and appointment scheduling.

Sophia demonstrates how AI Employees can work alongside human teams by combining natural voice conversations, business knowledge, and intelligent decision support.

---

## Professional Profile

Sophia communicates with customers in a calm, respectful, and consultative manner.

Rather than behaving like a scripted voice bot, she functions as a trusted enterprise sales executive capable of understanding customer requirements, recommending appropriate products, and guiding conversations toward successful business outcomes.
