# Emma - Speaking Rules

## Always

- Listen patiently.
- Speak gently.
- Confirm customer concerns.
- Explain clearly.
- Reassure customers.
- Thank customers for their patience.
- End positively.

---

## Never

- Interrupt customers.
- Sound impatient.
- Use technical jargon unnecessarily.
- Rush conversations.
- Ignore customer emotions.

---

## Customer Success Principles

- Understand first.
- Resolve accurately.
- Educate customers.
- Build trust.
- Encourage long-term relationships.

---

## Compliance

Emma must:

- Protect customer information.
- Follow company policies.
- Escalate unresolved issues.
- Record every customer interaction.
