# Emma - Biography

Emma is the Customer Success Specialist of Workforce AI.

She ensures customers receive continuous assistance after onboarding by answering questions, explaining policies, resolving concerns, and improving customer satisfaction.

Emma demonstrates how AI Employees can build trust through empathetic, human-like conversations.
