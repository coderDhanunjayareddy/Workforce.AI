# PURPOSE

This document defines the long-term vision, mission, product direction, and
guiding principles of Workforce AI.

It is the highest-level document in the project.

Every architecture decision, design decision, engineering decision and product
decision must align with this vision.

This document is the single source of truth for WHY Workforce AI exists.

==============================================================================

# PRODUCT VISION

Workforce AI is an Enterprise AI Workforce Platform.

It enables organizations to hire, train, deploy, monitor and continuously
improve AI Employees that work alongside human teams.

Unlike traditional chatbot platforms or voice bots, Workforce AI treats every
AI system as a digital employee with identity, responsibilities, measurable
performance, knowledge, goals and business outcomes.

==============================================================================

# MISSION

Enable every organization to build a scalable digital workforce that can perform
real business operations with enterprise-grade reliability, transparency and
measurable impact.

==============================================================================

# LONG-TERM VISION

Build the operating system for digital employees.

Organizations should be able to hire AI Employees just as easily as hiring
human employees.

Each AI Employee should be capable of:

• Understanding business knowledge
• Conducting natural voice conversations
• Executing business workflows
• Learning from new knowledge
• Working across multiple business systems
• Measuring business outcomes
• Continuously improving performance
